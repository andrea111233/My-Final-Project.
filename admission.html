B. Transferees Certificate of Transfer Credentials (CTC) or Certificate of Honorable Dismissal (CHD) Informative copy of Transcript of Records (for evaluation purposes only) Certificate of Good Moral Character Birth Certificate - PSA authenticated (photocopy) Medical Certificate for NURSING and MIDWIFERY NSO - Marriage Certificate (if married)
C. Admission to the College of Law Informative copy of Transcript of Records (baccalaureate with remarks of graduated; for evaluation purposes only) Certificate of General Weighted Average (80%) PhilSat Result Certificate of Transfer Credentials (CTC) or Certificate of Honorable Dismissal (CHD) C1 for Transferees only Special Order School File Birth Certificate - PSA authenticated (photocopy) NSO - Marriage Certificate (if married)
D. Professional School Certificate of Transfer Credentials (CTC) or Certificate of Honorable Dismissal (CHD) Informative copy of Transcript of Records (for evaluation purposes only) Special Order School File Birth Certificate - PSA authenticated (photocopy) NSO - Marriage Certificate (if married)
Foreign Students
A. Joint letter request addressed to the Commissioner from the authorized representative of the petitioning school and applicant, using the school letterhead with dry seal Duly accomplished CGAF (BI Form) for the Student Visa and Special Study Permit Photocopy of passport bio-page and latest admission with valid authorized stay and Bureau of Quarantine Stamp Notice of Acceptance of the applicant bearing a clear impression of the school's official dry seal Endorsement addressed to the Commissioner from the school for the conversion of the applicant's status signed by the School Registrar Certificate of Eligibility for Admission from Commission on Higher Education (CHED), in case of Medicine/Dentistry Medical Certificate issued by the Bureau of Quarantine and International Health Surveillance or a government medical institution with competence to certify that applicant is not afflicted with any dangerous, contagious or loathsome disease, and is mentally fit CHED endorsement for transfer and shifting of course, if applicable National Bureau of Investigation (NBI) clearance for a foreign student enrolling from bachelor's degree to post graduate; if stayed in the Philippines for more than 59 days Photocopy of BI school accreditation ID of the registrar or school representative BI Clearance Certificate 2 pcs. 2x2 ID picture Letter of Intent to study at The University of Mindanao Affidavit of Undertaking (for board course)
B. Red Ribbon Scholastic Records - High School Diploma and Transcript of Records Red Ribbon Birth Certificate Red Ribbon Police Clearance (issued from the country of origin) Red Ribbon Personal History Red Ribbon Proof of Support
Note:

Prepare each documents in 3 sets Place each set of requirements in a long brown folder with fastener
